Installation

Step 1

Download the Tabs for Good extension zip.

Download

Step 2

Unzip the "extensions" folder - remember where you put it!

Step 3

Open Chrome and go to "Manage Extensions"

Step 4

Turn on "Developer Mode"

Step 5

Click "Load unpacked"...

Step 5

... and select the folder you unzipped earlier.

Done!

Open a new tab - and discover Tabs for Good!
